02 · The refinement layer

Higgsfield. On every tier.

Every tier is shipped with a visual layer rendered through Higgsfield. Persistent character identity, cinematic stills, short-form video, voice. The site looks like it was art-directed because it was.

What Higgsfield does in the stack

Souls lock identity. Marketing Studio renders product shots. Generate-image and generate-video produce hero, gallery, and trailer assets. Voice presence comes from the same stack.

  • Soul, persistent face, body, energy
  • Marketing Studio, product shots, ad-style
  • generate_image, hero, gallery, social
  • generate_video, 5 to 30 second cinematics
  • media_upload, reference imagery in

How it lands per tier

  • T1, one Soul-locked hero, three gallery stills
  • T2, the above + scrolling gallery + one short video loop
  • T3, the above + one branded explainer video
  • T4, the above + cinematic trailer + character cameos
  • T5, the above + onboarding videos baked into the app

Identity locked once via Soul training, propagated across web, app, and social. One look. Five surfaces. Zero drift.
